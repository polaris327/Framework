OUR APPROACH
Increased Liquidity
Fractionalizing high unit cost assets and offering them to a global market will increase the potential for liquidity and market depth over time.
Digital and Programmable Tokens
Increased design space to control how real estate is bought and sold, and how ownership interest can be linked to real world utility and access.
Trading & Settlement
Unlike traditional settlement, using blockchain shortens the time and lowers the cost of trading and settlement, all in a transparent and auditable fashion.
1031 Exchange Alternative
An efficient process for real estate owners to 1031 exchange into up to 3 properties on the platform within the 45 day identification period and 180 day closing period. A structured, tax-efficient path to further diversify into other properties on the platform.
Automated Taxes and Compliance
Smart contract standards that ensure compliance with federal, state and local securities law and tax laws. AML / KYC occur off-chain, but are brought on-chain.
